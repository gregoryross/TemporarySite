I model biomolecules at the atomistic scale

I development and apply new techniques to predict the properties of biomolecules, particularly, the binding affinities of protein-drug complexes. Despite being one of the oldest problems in computational chemistry, this still remains a very challenging problem and a robust solution would be of great benefit to medicinal chemistry and pharmaceutical research. Most of my work involves simulating molecules using a combination of molecular dynamics and Monte Carlo. I am a research fellow in the Chodera lab .
